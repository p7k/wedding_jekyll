  1. Donate to Plan International!

    Consider a donation to Plan International, an organization dedicated to improving the lives of vulnerable children in developing countries. Young women in the developing world are more likely to be forced into an early marriage, be subjected to violence, be sold into the sex trade, or become infected with HIV. Often, girls face discrimination in their own homes. In many cases, they have limited access to a doctor or even a primary education (if they're able to go to school at all). The Because I am a Girl campaign is dedicated to empowering young women in these situations by supplying them with basic human rights such as food, healthcare, security and education.

    We encourage you to investigate the Because I am a Girl website, and if you are interested in donating to this cause you can make a check out to Plan International with a memo of Because I am a Girl and mail it to Lillian Dunlap at the address above. We are happy to handle final donations so that you can avoid being added to any unwanted mailing lists.

  2. Send us somewhere far, far away!

    Help fund the honeymoon. As many of you know, we're big travel lovers! We haven't chosen a honeymoon destination yet. There are just too many great places to choose from! We may go snorkeling in the Great Barrier Reef, or try a Safari in South Africa or Tanzania, or take a train trip through Europe! If you'd like to help us get to our destination, you can mail a check to Lillian or Danny at the above address.
