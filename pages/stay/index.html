There is no house big enough...

But here are some options

There is a hotel shortage in Marin County (San Rafael vicinity), so we suggest booking sooner rather than later, as most of the nearby hotels will sell out for our dates. San Francisco and the East Bay (Berkeley / Oakland) also make perfect sense for this trip.

We encourage everyone to check out alternative accommodation options, e.g. AirBnb, VRBO, and Homeaway. Hotels around The Bay are pricey - you might get a better deal this way, especially if you rent a larger house and share with others. Since these are 'by owner', new options continue to pop up all the time, as people skip town. But even the rentals that appear closer to the date will likely be more expensive, so we still encourage you to book sooner than later.

Let us know if you are open to shared arrangements, and we'll try to group you with other guests.
