Details

The wedding will begin at 3:00pm at the Falkirk Cultural Center in San Rafael, CA and will be followed by a cocktail hour, dinner and reception.

San Rafael is located 30 minutes North of San Francisco (over the golden gate bridge) (off of 101). Falkirk is essentially downtown, with abundant parking.

Falkirk Cultural Center is located at 1408 Mission Avenue in San Rafael, CA.

Attire

Fall Garden... Essentially, wear whatever you'd like. If you really aim to please, choose vintage fashion of from 1940's-1980s. Fall colors are encouraged. Gentlemen, here is your chance to dig out the brown suit.

As for practical concerns, Ceremony, cocktail hour and dinner will be outdoors, and there is a bit of an incline getting from one to the other. Ladies might be more comfortable in flats or wedges rather than a pointy heel. Also, the bay area tends to get chilly as the sun goes down, so a sweater or light jacket is a good idea for the evening. (Floridians, feel free to consult with the bride for weather wardrobe advice.) We have an indoor space for dancing and revelry as well.
