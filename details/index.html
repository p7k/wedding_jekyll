Details

The wedding will begin at 3:00pm at the Falkirk Cultural Center in San Rafael, CA and will be followed by a cocktail hour, dinner and reception.

Location

Falkirk is a gorgeous Victorian estate replete with cute gardens and vast lawns. It is in downtown San Rafael, which is 30 minutes North of San Francisco - across the Golden Gate Bridge. There will be plenty of on-site parking (see the map of Falkirk grounds below).

Fall Garden Attire

Full creative license is yours. Some ideas for inspiration: vintage fashion of 1940-1980's and fall colors; gentlemen, here is your chance to dig out the brown suit. As for practical concerns, Ceremony, cocktail hour and dinner will be outdoors, and there is a bit of an incline getting from one to the other. Ladies might be more comfortable in flats or wedges rather than pointy heels.

Climate

Our wedding ceremony and dinner will be outdoors. In October, the average high in San Rafael is 73 °F, and the average low is 51 °F. It will start to get chilly as the sun goes down (likely around the time we sit down to dinner), so a sweater or light jacket is a good idea for the evening. We have an indoor space for dancing and revelry as well.
